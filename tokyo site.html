Tokyo

Tokyo is one of the most populous cities in the world, with a metropolitan area housing over 37 million people.

The city has an extensive public transportation system, including one of the busiest subway networks globally, making it easy to navigate.

Tokyo is known for its blend of traditional and modern culture, featuring historic sites like the Senso-ji Temple alongside contemporary attractions like Akihabara and Shibuya.
